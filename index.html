What is Peacock Dating?

A modern courtship format: men speak on camera about who they are, what they value, their experiences, and what lights them up. Women can listen anonymously (camera optional) and choose privately who to connect with afterward. Matches are only shared on a mutual “yes.”

Hosted interview: A friendly host interviews each man to help him open up. The host may ask follow-ups (and a little light banter); men can decline any question.

How it works

  1. 1

    RSVP on Meetup and join the online event.

  2. 2

    The host interviews men in short rounds with light prompts.

  3. 3

    Men share stories, values, hobbies, and relationship insights. Speaking time is rationed so that all men on the call have a fair chance to speak. They may decline any question.

  4. 4

    Women can stay in the shade (camera off), listen comfortably, and DM questions to the host to ask on their behalf.

    If you want to ask verbally, you must enable both video and audio.

  5. 5

    After the event, women submit picks privately; only mutual “yes” = intro.

Tonight’s prompt ideas

  • Show & Tell: a hobby you love (share a photo).
  • A relationship insight you’ve learned.
  • A small ritual that makes your week better.
  • A project you’re proud of or a goal this year.

Why it works

Stories beat swipes. The format reduces pressure, spotlights character, and respects privacy and consent.

For Men

  • • Camera on for the full event.
  • • Be brave. Share who you are—values, hobbies, lessons learned.
  • • Hosted interview format: you can decline any question.
  • • Expect light jokes/banter from the host—stay good-humoured.
  • • Your speaking time is rationed so everyone has a chance to share. Keep it concise and engaging.

For Women

  • • Camera optional; you may remain anonymous (stay “in the shade”).
  • • DM questions to the host; they’ll ask on your behalf.
  • • If you want to ask a question yourself, you must turn on both video and audio.
  • • Listen comfortably; note anyone you’d like to meet.
  • • After the event, submit picks privately. Mutual “yes” = intro.

For the Host

  • • Keep speakers comfortable; ask follow-ups to surface personality.
  • • Read selected questions from women (anonymous by default).
  • • Maintain pace, fairness, and a respectful tone.
  • • Step in or remove participants who break rules.

Eligibility, Rules & Safety

  • • Ages 18+ only.
  • • Respectful conduct; no harassment, discrimination, or doxxing.
  • • No screenshots or recordings.
  • • No contact details shared during the event; intros only on mutual consent.
  • • The host may skip or rephrase questions; men may decline questions.
  • • Spoken questions from women require both video and audio enabled; otherwise please DM the host to ask on your behalf.
  • • Hosts may remove anyone who breaks the rules.

FAQ

Is this like a dating app?

No. It’s a live, host-guided interview with clear rules and mutual-consent matching only.

Do women have to show their face?

No. Women can attend with cameras off and remain anonymous if they prefer.

How can women ask questions?

DM your question to the host during the event; they’ll read it to the speaker. If you want to ask directly, you must switch on both your camera and mic.

Can men refuse a question?

Yes. Men may decline any question. The host keeps things respectful and light, including gentle jokes.

How do matches work?

After the event, women privately submit picks. We only introduce pairs that both say “yes.”

What should men prepare?

A short intro, a story, and a hobby or project you care about. Authenticity > performance.
